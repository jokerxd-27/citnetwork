About Our Center

The Center of Information Technology (CIT) is a pioneering institution dedicated to advancing Pakistan's digital landscape. We serve as a hub for research, education, and collaboration, bringing together the brightest minds from academia, industry, and government.

We are committed to creating sustainable technological solutions that address local challenges and unlock new opportunities for growth and development.

Location: Islamabad, Pakistan

Our Key Focus Areas

We concentrate our efforts on pivotal domains that promise the greatest impact.

Technology & Startups

Nurturing a vibrant ecosystem for tech startups, from ideation to scaling, through mentorship and funding opportunities.

Education & Training

Offering cutting-edge courses and training programs in AI, Cybersecurity, and Cloud Computing to build a skilled workforce.

Key Local Industries

Driving digital adoption in key industries like textiles and surgical goods to enhance productivity and global competitiveness.

National Digital Vision

Aligning our work with the national agenda for a digitally empowered Pakistan.

Digital Pakistan Vision

We actively support the Digital Pakistan vision by focusing on its core pillars. Our projects are designed to contribute directly to these national goals.

  • Access & Connectivity for all citizens.
  • Digital Infrastructure development.
  • E-Government services implementation.
  • Digital Skills & Literacy enhancement.

Government Schemes & Support

We help businesses and individuals leverage government schemes designed to promote technological growth and entrepreneurship.

  • Prime Minister's Youth Business & Agriculture Loan Scheme.
  • National Incubation Center (NIC) Programs.
  • IGNITE National Technology Fund grants.
