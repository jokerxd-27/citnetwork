About Our Center

Established to serve as a hub for technological advancement and digital literacy, the Center of Information Technology is dedicated to fostering innovation and growth. We believe in harnessing the power of technology to solve local challenges and create new opportunities for our citizens.

Located in the heart of our vibrant community, we are strategically positioned to support local industries, educational institutions, and aspiring tech professionals.

Location: Main Tech Park, Digital City, Pakistan

Embracing Digital Pakistan

We are fully aligned with the national 'Digital Pakistan' vision. Our center actively implements key initiatives to enhance connectivity, improve digital infrastructure, and promote e-governance at the local level.

Our implementation focuses on citizen training, digital literacy campaigns, and providing access to government services online, ensuring that the benefits of a digital nation reach every individual in our community.

Government Support

We work closely with federal programs like the Prime Minister's Youth Program to offer subsidized training and skill development opportunities in the tech sector, fostering entrepreneurship and job creation.

In partnership with provincial authorities, we facilitate access to schemes aimed at empowering SMEs with digital tools, helping them grow their business and compete in the modern economy.
