Welcome to the Center of Information Technology

Bringing Digital Transformation to Block B, Near Municipal Library, Turbat, Balochistan, Pakistan

Our center is dedicated to fostering innovation, providing access to technology, and empowering individuals and businesses within our community. We are committed to bridging the digital divide and driving sustainable development through technology and education.

About Our Center

Located at: Block B, Near Municipal Library, Turbat, Balochistan, Pakistan

The Center plays a pivotal role in supporting local development by providing essential technological resources, educational programs, and a collaborative environment for innovation. We strive to equip our community with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the digital age.

Our Key Focus Areas

Tourism & Cultural Heritage

We aim to promote tourism and celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Balochistan through digital platforms and innovative initiatives.

Key Tourist Attractions: Princess of Hope Rock Formation (25.2651, 63.7450) Gwadar Port Viewpoint (25.1264, 62.3257)

Upcoming Events/Festivals: Baloch Culture Day – March Gwadar Music and Heritage Festival – November

Agriculture & Local Products

We support the agricultural sector by providing access to technology and promoting sustainable farming practices that enhance productivity and improve the livelihoods of local farmers.

Major Crops & Farming Types: Dates, Bananas, Fodder Crops Traditional Canal Irrigation, Date Palm Farming

Agri-Products for Sale: Dry & Fresh Dates Banana Chips and Jam Hand-packed Date Syrup

Technology & Innovation Hub

Our center serves as a hub for technology and innovation, providing resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities for startups and aspiring entrepreneurs.

Existing Technology Usage: Solar desalination units for irrigation SMS-based crop alert system Solar drying tunnels for date preservation

Local Tech Startups & Solutions: DesertTech Labs – Sustainable tech for arid farming (contact@deserttech.pk)

Skill Development & Training

We offer a variety of skill development and training programs designed to equip individuals with the skills they need to succeed in the modern workforce.

Available Courses & Workshops: Digital Marketing in Urdu Solar Energy Installation Basics Date Palm Processing & Export Techniques

Associated Training Centers: Baloch Digital Center, Turbat Technical Training Institute, Gwadar

Industry & Manufacturing

We support the growth of local industry and manufacturing by providing access to technology, training, and market opportunities.

Key Industries: Date Processing Units Handicrafts (Textiles, Embroidery)

Major Manufactured Products: Export-quality Dates Handwoven Balochi Ralli Quilts Traditional Embroidered Dresses

Embracing Digital Pakistan

Our center is committed to supporting the Digital Pakistan vision by promoting digital literacy, providing access to technology, and fostering innovation within our community.

Adopted Initiatives: Access & Connectivity, Digital Infrastructure, E-Government, Digital Skilling & Literacy, Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Implementation Details: Helpdesk for NADRA e-services and passport applications Training 100+ women in mobile freelancing Startup grant program launched for local crafts export

Government Support & Adoption

We actively leverage government schemes and initiatives to support our community and promote sustainable development.

Adopted Federal Schemes: Kamyab Jawan – Local youth trained in trades Ehsaas Undergraduate Scholarship – 20+ beneficiaries supported

Adopted Provincial Schemes: Balochistan Skills Development Program – Vocational training Balochistan Youth Empowerment Program – Digital learning drive
