Welcome to the Center of Information Technology

Bringing Digital Transformation to Suite #402, IT Tower, University Road, Gulshan-e-Iqbal, Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan

Our mission is to empower the community through technology and innovation, fostering sustainable development and economic growth. We envision a future where digital literacy is accessible to all, creating opportunities and bridging the digital divide.

About Our Center

Located at: Suite #402, IT Tower, University Road, Gulshan-e-Iqbal, Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan

The Center of Information Technology plays a vital role in the local development of Karachi. We strive to enhance digital literacy, support local businesses, and promote technological innovation within the community.

Our Key Focus Areas

We are dedicated to promoting tourism and preserving cultural heritage through digital initiatives and innovative solutions.

Key Tourist Attractions: Clifton Beach (24.8138, 67.0305) Mohatta Palace Museum (24.8152, 67.0212) Karachi Zoo (24.8705, 67.0330)

Upcoming Events/Festivals: Karachi Eat Festival – January Sindh Literature Festival – February Pakistan Day Parade – March 23

Our center provides support to local farmers and agricultural businesses, helping them adopt sustainable practices and promote their products.

Major Crops & Farming Types: Rice, Chillies, Sugarcane (from interior Sindh partnerships) Urban hydroponics pilot project at center rooftop

Agri-Products for Sale: Hydroponic Lettuce & Spinach Sindhi Pickles & Spices Local Rice Packs

We integrate technology into various sectors to drive innovation and improve efficiency within our community.

Existing Technology Usage: Learning Management System (LMS) for remote students QR-based attendance system Rooftop solar powering 70% of center's electricity

Local Tech Startups & Solutions: CodeHarbor – Freelance skill accelerator for youth (hello@codeharbor.pk) AgriStack Karachi – Data-driven agriculture dashboards

We offer various courses and workshops to equip individuals with essential skills for the digital age, promoting employment and entrepreneurship.

Available Courses & Workshops: Full Stack Web Development (HTML, JS, React) Freelancing & Portfolio Building Blockchain Basics & Use Cases

Associated Training Centers: Karachi Digital Empowerment Hub (KDEH) Sindh Technical Education Board (STEB) Learning Campus

We are committed to supporting local industries and manufacturing businesses by providing access to resources and promoting innovation.

Key Industries: Leather Goods Manufacturing Food Packaging & Processing

Major Manufactured Products: Leather Wallets and Jackets Packaged Spices and Pickled Vegetables

Embracing Digital Pakistan

The Center of Information Technology is dedicated to supporting the national vision of a Digital Pakistan by implementing initiatives that promote digital literacy and technological advancement.

Adopted Initiatives: Access & Connectivity, Digital Infrastructure, E-Government, Digital Skilling & Literacy, Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Implementation Details: Launched 100+ hours of free online IT courses Helpdesk for NADRA, vehicle verification, and online challans Karachi TechFest hosted by the center under Ignite partnership 200+ students trained under PM’s Digital Skills Program
