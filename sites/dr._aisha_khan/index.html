Welcome to the Center of Information Technology

Bringing Digital Transformation to CIT Center Building, 15-B, Industrial Area, Phase IV, Islamabad

Our center is dedicated to empowering the local community through digital literacy, technological innovation, and sustainable development initiatives. We strive to bridge the digital divide and foster a thriving ecosystem for growth and prosperity.

About Our Center

Located at: CIT Center Building, 15-B, Industrial Area, Phase IV, Islamabad

The CIT Center plays a vital role in local development by providing resources, training, and support for individuals and businesses. We aim to promote innovation, create opportunities, and contribute to the overall well-being of our community.

Our Key Focus Areas

Tourism & Cultural Heritage

We actively promote tourism and preserve cultural heritage in the region by supporting local artisans, showcasing historical sites, and organizing cultural events.

Key Tourist Attractions: Lok Virsa Museum (GPS: 33.7293° N, 73.0416° E), Faisal Mosque (GPS: 33.7297° N, 73.0374° E), Margalla Hills National Park (GPS: 33.7380° N, 73.0470° E)

Upcoming Events/Festivals: Pakistan National Folk Festival (Lok Mela) - October, Islamabad Literature Festival - April

Agriculture & Local Products

We provide support to local farmers and promote sustainable agricultural practices to enhance productivity and improve livelihoods.

Major Crops & Farming Types: Wheat (rabi), Maize (kharif), Sugarcane, Potatoes

Agri-Products for Sale: Organic honey, Dairy products (cheese, yogurt), Fresh vegetables (tomatoes, cucumbers), Basmati rice

Technology & Innovation Hub

We foster technological advancements by providing access to resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities for innovators and entrepreneurs.

Existing Technology Usage: Smart irrigation systems using IoT, Weather monitoring stations, Online farmer advisory portals, Drone-based crop health monitoring.

Local Tech Startups & Solutions: AgriTech Solutions (AI-driven crop management, Contact: info@agritech.pk), EduSphere (Ed-tech platform for rural areas, Contact: support@edusphere.co)

Skill Development & Training

We offer a variety of courses and workshops to equip individuals with the skills they need to succeed in today's rapidly evolving job market.

Available Courses & Workshops: Digital Marketing Fundamentals, Web Development (HTML/CSS/JS), Advanced Excel for Business, Modern Hydroponics Techniques, Solar Panel Installation & Maintenance

Associated Training Centers: National Incubation Center (NIC) Islamabad, Comsats University Skill Development Center, TEVTA Training Institute Rawalpindi

Industry & Manufacturing

We support local industries and manufacturers by providing resources, training, and access to markets to help them grow and thrive.

Key Industries: IT Services, Textile Weaving, Handicrafts (Pottery, Embroidery), Small-scale Food Processing

Major Manufactured Products: Software solutions, Embroidered fabrics, Ceramic pottery, Packaged fruit juices

Embracing Digital Pakistan

We are fully committed to supporting the Digital Pakistan vision by promoting digital literacy, fostering innovation, and enabling access to technology for all.

Adopted Initiatives: Access & Connectivity, Digital Infrastructure, E-Government, Digital Skilling & Literacy, Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Implementation Details: Implemented free Wi-Fi zones in public spaces, developed a local e-governance portal for permits and registrations, launched digital literacy workshops for small businesses, and established a startup incubator focusing on agri-tech solutions.

Government Support & Adoption

We actively leverage government schemes and programs to provide additional support and resources to our community members.

Adopted Federal Schemes: Kamyab Jawan Program (youth entrepreneurship loans), Ehsaas Program (social protection and poverty alleviation), Prime Minister's Youth Skills Development Program

Adopted Provincial Schemes: Punjab Rozgar Scheme (small business loans for youth), E-Rozgaar Training Program (freelancing skills), Punjab Green Development Program
