About Our Center

The Center of Information Technology is a hub for innovation, dedicated to accelerating local development through digital literacy and technological advancement. We provide the tools and knowledge necessary for our community to thrive in the modern world.

Located in the heart of Tech Valley, we serve as a pivotal point for collaboration between academia, industry, and government to drive forward-thinking initiatives.

Embracing Digital Pakistan

Our center is a key partner in the national "Digital Pakistan" vision. We are actively involved in deploying initiatives aimed at improving digital infrastructure, increasing connectivity, and promoting e-governance at the local level.

Through focused implementation strategies, we ensure that the benefits of digital transformation reach every citizen, from online public services to digital payment systems, creating a more inclusive and efficient society.

Government Support

We facilitate access to various government schemes designed to bolster the tech ecosystem. Federal programs provide grants for tech startups and IT exports, fostering innovation on a national scale.

Provincial schemes offer localized support, including subsidized training programs for youth, tax incentives for new IT businesses, and funding for technology parks, directly contributing to regional economic growth.
