Welcome to the Center of Information Technology

Bringing Digital Transformation to Rawalpindi

Our center is dedicated to fostering technological growth and innovation within our community. We strive to empower individuals and businesses through access to cutting-edge resources, training, and support.

About Our Center

Located at: Rawalpindi

We play a vital role in local development by providing resources, training, and support to individuals and businesses, fostering technological growth and innovation within Rawalpindi.

Our Key Focus Areas

Tourism & Cultural Heritage

We are committed to promoting tourism and preserving our cultural heritage through digital solutions and innovative strategies.

Agriculture & Local Products

We support the agriculture sector by providing farmers with access to technology and training to improve productivity and market access.

Technology & Innovation Hub

We strive to create a vibrant technology and innovation hub by fostering collaboration, providing resources, and supporting startups.

Skill Development & Training

We offer a range of skill development and training programs to equip individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in the digital age.

Industry & Manufacturing

We support the local industrial sector by promoting innovation, improving efficiency, and facilitating access to new markets.

Embracing Digital Pakistan

We are committed to supporting the Digital Pakistan vision by promoting digital literacy, fostering innovation, and facilitating access to technology for all.

Government Support & Adoption

We actively leverage government programs and schemes to support our initiatives and provide access to resources for our community.

Adopted Federal Schemes: Kamyab Jawan Program (Startup Loans) Ehsaas Kafalat Program Prime Minister's E-Learning Program

Adopted Provincial Schemes: Punjab Rozgar Scheme E-Rozgaar Program Hunarmand Nojawan Program
