About Our Center

We are a pivotal institution dedicated to advancing technological proficiency and digital integration within our local economy.

Established to serve as a hub for learning, collaboration, and innovation, the CIT Center provides resources, training, and support to individuals, businesses, and public sector entities. We aim to create a self-sustaining tech ecosystem that drives growth and opportunity for all.
