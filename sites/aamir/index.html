About Our Center

We are a forward-thinking institution dedicated to advancing information technology and digital literacy within our community. Our goal is to create a hub for learning, innovation, and collaboration.

Located in the heart of our vibrant city, we provide state-of-the-art facilities and expert-led training to bridge the technology gap and unlock new opportunities for everyone. Our location allows us to serve the community effectively.

Location: Mianwali, Punjab, Pakistan

Embracing Digital Pakistan

We are a proud partner in the national "Digital Pakistan" vision, working to implement key initiatives at a local level. Our efforts focus on enhancing digital infrastructure, promoting e-governance, and ensuring digital inclusion for all citizens.

Implementation Focus: We are actively launching training programs and workshops aligned with DP pillars to equip our community with the necessary skills for a digital future.

Government Support

We collaborate with government bodies to bring beneficial schemes and programs to our area. These initiatives provide crucial support for aspiring entrepreneurs, students, and professionals in the tech sector.

Available Schemes: We provide guidance and access to Federal youth loan programs and Provincial e-rozgaar training schemes, helping to turn innovative ideas into successful ventures.
