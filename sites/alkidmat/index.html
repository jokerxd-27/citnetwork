About Our Center

The Center of Information Technology (CIT) is dedicated to driving digital transformation and capacity building in our local area. We aim to be a hub for innovation, education, and community engagement in technology.

Our efforts are focused on bridging the digital divide, providing access to modern technological resources, and cultivating a skilled workforce ready for the challenges of the digital age.
