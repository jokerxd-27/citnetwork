Welcome to the Center of Information Technology

Bringing Digital Transformation to Street #12, Near Railway Station, Swabi, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan

Our center is committed to fostering innovation and driving digital literacy across the community. We aim to empower individuals and businesses with the skills and resources they need to thrive in the digital age.

About Our Center

Located at: Street #12, Near Railway Station, Swabi, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan

Our center plays a vital role in the local development by providing technology resources, training programs, and support for local businesses. We are dedicated to improving the quality of life and boosting economic opportunities in our region.

Our Key Focus Areas

Tourism & Cultural Heritage

We aim to promote tourism and preserve the rich cultural heritage of our region through technology integration and sustainable tourism practices.

Key Tourist Attractions: Kund Park (34.0586, 72.6710) River Indus Boating Point (34.0491, 72.6332)

Upcoming Events/Festivals: Local Basant Festival – February KP Cultural Day – September

Agriculture & Local Products

We provide support to local farmers and promote sustainable agricultural practices to enhance productivity and ensure food security in our community.

Major Crops & Farming Types: Tobacco, Maize, Wheat Traditional Field Farming and Drip Irrigation

Agri-Products for Sale: Dried Tobacco Leaves Organic Maize Flour Local Pickled Vegetables

Technology & Innovation Hub

We foster technology integration across industries and create a hub for innovation and digital solutions to solve local problems and improve efficiency.

Existing Technology Usage: GPS-enabled tractors Mobile advisory apps for farmers Solar-powered water pumps

Local Tech Startups & Solutions: KisanLink Swabi – Digital platform for farmer-to-buyer selling (kisanlink@swabi.pk)

Skill Development & Training

We offer courses and workshops to equip individuals with the skills they need to excel in the digital economy and create better opportunities for themselves and their families.

Available Courses & Workshops: Freelancing with Fiverr & Upwork Python Programming for Beginners Post-Harvest Crop Management

Associated Training Centers: DigiLink Training Center, Swabi Swabi Government Vocational Institute

Industry & Manufacturing

We support local industries by facilitating the adoption of modern manufacturing technologies and promoting efficient supply chain management.

Key Industries: Tobacco Processing Sports Goods Manufacturing

Major Manufactured Products: Dried Tobacco & Cigarette Paper Hand-stitched Footballs

Embracing Digital Pakistan

Our center is committed to supporting the Digital Pakistan vision by promoting digital literacy, enhancing connectivity, and fostering a digital-friendly environment for all.

Adopted Initiatives: Access & Connectivity, E-Government, Digital Skilling & Literacy

Implementation Details: Local residents use online portals for CNIC verification and bills Conducted 3 digital literacy bootcamps Setup DigiCorner for youth with internet access and e-learning support

Government Support & Adoption

We actively leverage government schemes and programs to enhance our initiatives and provide additional resources to our community.

Adopted Federal Schemes: Local residents use online portals for CNIC verification and bills Conducted 3 digital literacy bootcamps Setup DigiCorner for youth with internet access and e-learning support

Adopted Provincial Schemes: KP Youth Digital Training – Conducted under Khyber Pakhtunkhwa IT Board KP Hunarmand Scheme – Enrolled 60+ youth for technical skills
