Welcome to the Center of Information Technology

Bringing Digital Transformation to DigitalVision Center, G-11 Markaz, Islamabad, Pakistan

Our center is dedicated to empowering individuals and businesses through technology, fostering innovation, and driving sustainable growth within our community.

About Our Center

Located at: DigitalVision Center, G-11 Markaz, Islamabad, Pakistan

We play a crucial role in local development by providing resources, training, and support to help people and organizations thrive in the digital age.

Our Key Focus Areas

Tourism & Cultural Heritage

We promote tourism and preserve our cultural heritage by leveraging technology to showcase local attractions and historical sites.

Agriculture & Local Products

We support the agriculture sector by providing access to information, resources, and innovative technologies to improve productivity and promote local products.

Technology & Innovation Hub

Our center serves as a technology and innovation hub, fostering collaboration and providing resources for startups and entrepreneurs to develop cutting-edge solutions.

Skill Development & Training

We offer a range of skill development and training programs to equip individuals with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in today's competitive job market.

Industry & Manufacturing

We support local industries and manufacturing businesses by providing access to technology, resources, and training to improve efficiency and competitiveness.

Embracing Digital Pakistan

We are committed to supporting the national vision of a Digital Pakistan by promoting technology adoption and digital literacy across all sectors of society.

Government Support & Adoption

We actively leverage various government programs and initiatives to provide additional support and resources to our community.

Adopted Federal Schemes: Kamyab Jawan Program (Skill training & startup loans) Ehsaas Kafalat (registered families assisted)

Adopted Provincial Schemes: Punjab E-Rozgaar (freelancer training) Punjab Rozgar Scheme (startup microfinancing)
