Welcome to the Center of Information Technology

Bringing Digital Transformation to Plot #45, Main GT Road, Near Metro Station, Gujranwala, Punjab, Pakistan

Our mission is to foster innovation and empower the local community through technology and digital literacy programs. We envision a future where everyone has access to the tools and knowledge they need to thrive in the digital age.

About Our Center

Located at: Plot #45, Main GT Road, Near Metro Station, Gujranwala, Punjab, Pakistan

The Center of Information Technology plays a crucial role in the local development by providing resources, training, and support to individuals and businesses alike. We strive to bridge the digital divide and create opportunities for economic growth and social progress.

Our Key Focus Areas

Tourism & Cultural Heritage

We promote local tourism by highlighting cultural landmarks and attracting visitors to experience the rich history and traditions of our region.

Key Tourist Attractions: Chenab River Viewpoint (32.1652° N, 74.1873° E) Gujranwala Clock Tower (32.1553° N, 74.1872° E) Sheesh Mahal Park (32.1489° N, 74.1967° E)

Upcoming Events/Festivals: Gujranwala Food Festival – March Eid Cultural Mela – April Independence Day Parade – August 14 Winter Crafts Fair – December

Agriculture & Local Products

We support local farmers and promote sustainable agricultural practices to ensure food security and economic prosperity in our community.

Major Crops & Farming Types: Wheat, Rice, Sugarcane Organic vegetable farms Poultry and dairy farms

Agri-Products for Sale: Organic spinach and tomatoes Pure desi ghee Handmade pickles and processed yogurt

Technology & Innovation Hub

We foster a culture of innovation and technology adoption by providing resources and support to local startups and tech enthusiasts.

Existing Technology Usage: Use of drone technology for pesticide spraying Solar irrigation in outer villages IoT sensors for greenhouse farming

Local Tech Startups & Solutions: AgroSense Pvt. Ltd. – Builds smart agriculture sensors (Contact: info@agrosense.pk) CleanSolar – Community-based solar panel solutions (Facebook: fb.com/cleansolarpk)

Skill Development & Training

We empower individuals with the skills they need to succeed in the modern workforce through a variety of training programs and workshops.

Industry & Manufacturing

We support local industries and manufacturers by providing resources and promoting innovation to enhance productivity and competitiveness.

Embracing Digital Pakistan

We are committed to supporting the national vision of a Digital Pakistan by promoting digital literacy, innovation, and technology adoption in our community.

Adopted Initiatives: Access & Connectivity, Digital Infrastructure, E-Government, Digital Skilling & Literacy, Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Implementation Details: Provided free Wi-Fi access in community centers Trained over 300 youth in freelancing through Digital Skilling program Launched an e-services portal for local complaints and utilities Supported two startups under Ignite funding
