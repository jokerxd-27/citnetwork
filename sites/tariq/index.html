Welcome to the Center of Information Technology

Bringing Digital Transformation to Plot #22, Satellite Town, Muzaffarabad, Azad Jammu & Kashmir, Pakistan

Our center is dedicated to empowering our community through access to technology, education, and innovative solutions. We strive to bridge the digital divide and create opportunities for growth and development in the region.

About Our Center

Located at: Plot #22, Satellite Town, Muzaffarabad, Azad Jammu & Kashmir, Pakistan

The Center of Information Technology plays a crucial role in local development by providing access to technology, training, and resources. We aim to foster innovation, entrepreneurship, and digital literacy in our community.

Our Key Focus Areas

Tourism & Cultural Heritage

We are committed to promoting sustainable tourism and preserving our rich cultural heritage. Our initiatives focus on showcasing the beauty of our region and supporting local artisans and cultural practitioners.

Key Tourist Attractions: Pir Chinasi Hill Station (34.3525, 73.5866) Neelum Valley Viewpoint (34.4951, 74.0152)

Upcoming Events/Festivals: Kashmir Cultural Heritage Day – September Azadi Festival – August 14

Agriculture & Local Products

We support local farmers and promote sustainable agricultural practices. Our initiatives aim to enhance productivity, improve market access, and promote the unique agricultural products of our region.

Major Crops & Farming Types: Maize, Apple Orchards, Walnut Trees Terrace and hillside farming

Agri-Products for Sale: Dry Walnuts and Apples Apple Jam and Chutneys Organic Maize Flour

Technology & Innovation Hub

We are creating a vibrant technology and innovation hub to foster creativity, entrepreneurship, and digital literacy. Our initiatives include providing access to technology, training programs, and networking opportunities.

Existing Technology Usage: Cold storage units powered by solar GIS mapping of land plots ICT labs for students in remote areas

Local Tech Startups & Solutions: EcoGrid Labs – Clean energy solutions for hill agriculture (info@ecogridlabs.pk)

Skill Development & Training

We offer a variety of skill development and training programs to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in the digital age. Our courses are designed to be practical, relevant, and accessible to all.

Available Courses & Workshops: Intro to Graphic Design IT for Rural Entrepreneurs Beekeeping and Honey Packaging

Associated Training Centers: VisionNext Center, Muzaffarabad AJ&K Technical & Vocational Institute

Industry & Manufacturing

We support the development of local industries and manufacturing sectors. Our initiatives aim to enhance productivity, improve product quality, and promote market access for local businesses.

Key Industries: Cottage Textile & Wool Industry Handmade Wooden Crafts

Major Manufactured Products: Wool Shawls and Scarves Hand-carved Wooden Utensils and Souvenirs

Embracing Digital Pakistan

We are committed to supporting the Digital Pakistan vision by leveraging technology to transform our community and create opportunities for growth and development.

Adopted Initiatives: Access & Connectivity, Digital Infrastructure, E-Government, Digital Skilling & Literacy, Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Implementation Details: Satellite internet installed in upper valley schools Community women trained to sell crafts on Daraz & Facebook Marketplace Youth Innovation Fair held with 10 tech prototypes displayed

Government Support & Adoption

We actively leverage government schemes and programs to support our initiatives and empower our community.

Adopted Federal Schemes: Kamyab Jawan – 3 startups funded in IT and Food Ehsaas Undergraduate Scholarship – 15 local students supported

Adopted Provincial Schemes: AJK IT Training Scheme – 80+ trained in basic computing AJK Women Empowerment Grant – Craft workshops conducted
