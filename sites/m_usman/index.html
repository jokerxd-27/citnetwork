About Our Center

Located at: rwp

Our center plays a vital role in local development by providing resources, training, and support to help individuals and businesses thrive in the digital age.

Our Key Focus Areas

Tourism & Cultural Heritage

We support the tourism sector by promoting cultural heritage and developing digital tools to enhance the visitor experience.

Agriculture & Local Products

We provide support to the agricultural sector by promoting sustainable farming practices and developing digital platforms for local product distribution.

Technology & Innovation Hub

Our center serves as a hub for technology and innovation, fostering collaboration and providing resources to startups and entrepreneurs.

Skill Development & Training

We offer a variety of courses and skill development programs to equip individuals with the knowledge and abilities needed to succeed in today's digital economy.

Industry & Manufacturing

We support the local industrial sector by promoting innovation, providing access to technology, and fostering partnerships with manufacturers.

Embracing Digital Pakistan

We are committed to supporting the Digital Pakistan vision by promoting technology adoption, digital literacy, and innovation across all sectors.

Government Support & Adoption

Our center actively leverages government programs and schemes to provide maximum support and resources to our community.

Adopted Federal Schemes: Kamyab Jawan Program (Skill training & startup loans) Ehsaas Kafalat (registered families assisted)

Adopted Provincial Schemes: Punjab E-Rozgaar (freelancer training) Punjab Rozgar Scheme (startup microfinancing)
