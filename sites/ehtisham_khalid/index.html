About Our Center

The CIT Center stands as a beacon of technological advancement, driving local development and empowerment. We believe in harnessing the power of information technology to solve real-world problems and create opportunities for all.

Located at 123 Tech Avenue, Innovation City, Pakistan, our state-of-the-art facility provides a collaborative environment for learning, innovation, and networking. Join us in shaping the digital future.

Embracing Digital Pakistan

CIT actively supports the Digital Pakistan initiative through various programs, including digital literacy campaigns, e-governance workshops, and fostering tech entrepreneurship. We believe in leveraging technology to bridge the digital divide and create a knowledge-based economy.

Our implementation focuses on skill development for youth, promoting digital payment solutions, and collaborating with government agencies to streamline digital services. We provide platforms for citizens to access online resources and participate in the digital ecosystem.

Government Support

CIT also facilitates access to various government schemes aimed at promoting technology and economic growth:

Federal Schemes:

  • Prime Minister's Youth Program
  • Kamyab Jawan Program (Tech Skills)
  • Ehsaas Program (Digital Financial Inclusion)
