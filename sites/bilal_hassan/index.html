Welcome to the Center of Information Technology

Bringing Digital Transformation to Near Govt. College, Main Road, Chiniot, Punjab, Pakistan

Our mission is to empower our community through access to technology and digital literacy, fostering innovation and economic growth in the region. We envision a future where everyone has the skills and opportunities to thrive in the digital age.

About Our Center

Located at: Near Govt. College, Main Road, Chiniot, Punjab, Pakistan

Our center plays a vital role in the local development by providing resources, training, and support for individuals and businesses to adopt and leverage technology for growth and sustainability.

Our Key Focus Areas

Tourism & Cultural Heritage

We are dedicated to promoting local tourism and preserving our rich cultural heritage through digital platforms and innovative initiatives.

Key Tourist Attractions: Chenab River Viewpoint (31.7166, 72.9789) Historic Umar Hayat Mahal Library (31.7198, 72.9782)

Upcoming Events/Festivals: Local Spring Cultural Festival – March Eid Mela & Craft Bazaar – July

Agriculture & Local Products

We provide support and resources for local farmers to improve their practices and promote their products to wider markets through digital technology.

Major Crops & Farming Types: Sugarcane, Wheat, and Mango Orchards Tube-well and Canal-based Farming

Agri-Products for Sale: Mango Pulp & Juices Locally made jaggery (gur) Fresh wheat flour

Technology & Innovation Hub

We aim to be a hub for technological advancements, providing access to resources and training for individuals and businesses to innovate and thrive in the digital age.

Existing Technology Usage: Mobile apps for market price checking Water-efficient drip irrigation systems Solar tube-well installations

Local Tech Startups & Solutions: AgriSmart Devices – IoT soil moisture sensors (Contact: info@agrismart.pk)

Skill Development & Training

We offer a variety of courses and workshops to enhance digital literacy and equip individuals with the skills needed for today's job market.

Available Courses & Workshops: WordPress Web Development Basic Excel & Office Tools Sustainable Crop Practices

Associated Training Centers: FutureTech Learning Center, Chiniot TEVTA Skill Center, Chiniot

Industry & Manufacturing

We support local industries and manufacturers by providing access to resources and training to improve efficiency and expand their reach through technology.

Key Industries: Wood Furniture Manufacturing Textile Stitching Units

Major Manufactured Products: Hand-carved Sheesham Furniture Cotton Bed Sheets and Pillow Covers

Embracing Digital Pakistan

We are committed to supporting the Digital Pakistan vision by promoting digital literacy, innovation, and entrepreneurship in our community.

Adopted Initiatives: Digital Infrastructure, Digital Skilling & Literacy, Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Implementation Details: Public Wi-Fi zone installed at the community hall Partnered with NAVTTC for digital skill training Hosted a local startup pitch day to encourage innovation

Government Support & Adoption

We actively leverage government programs and schemes to support our community and promote economic growth.

Adopted Federal Schemes: Kamyab Jawan Program – One participant secured startup funds Ehsaas Emergency Cash – Community registration support provided

Adopted Provincial Schemes: Punjab Rozgar Scheme – 3 small loans approved E-Rozgaar Program – 45 youth trained for freelance work
